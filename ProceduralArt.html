Procedural Art

Computers provide quite an interesting way to creating art. It's a form of mixing the precision of a logical system with the inestability of the human mind, expressed in the form of an algorithm.

Procedural art is, in general terms, a form of digital or computer-generated art created using algorithms, computer programs, or systematic processes to generate images, animations, or other visual or auditory elements. It is a type of generative art where the artist defines a set of rules or procedures that dictate how the artwork should be generated, but the actual outcome can be unpredictable or vary each time the program is run.
