Game Design

My father passed away a this year. His favourite games were from the SEGA Genesis and NES, with titles like Gain Ground and some simple titles like Baseball. When we played together, I felt connected with him in a way that I didn't know it was possible, making me feel emotionally connected to him while having some friendly competition. Videogames made possible for me to connect with my father many times, and that's how I learned to love videogames. During my last trip to Mexico, and one of the last times I saw my father, we found our old NES console while cleaning his room and we spent the late evening playing his favourite game of Baseball.

Gardens of Caprona

Join a crew of scientists and explore the biodiversity of a new world in this single-player, alien farming simulator. Plant new species from your inventory to gather income and invite new creatures to visit your garden. The unique combination of items you decide to plant will determine which creatures you encounter. Expand your inventory and acquire new seeds to cultivate in your garden. As you attract more species, a cascade of biological interactions that include predation and mating will be revealed. Gather resources and discover the full spectrum of species thriving in the world of Caprona!

Artificia Armata

Welcome to the resistance! We live in a dystopic world where humanity is threatened by the very same machines that we built to protect us. Make your way through waves of enemies in this single-player, top-down tower defense game, defeating a final boss for each level. Upgrade your defense weaponry, and survive the 9 levels that lead to the Articia Armada system.
Available on Steam

Evolution in Action

Meet the pinks. These cute herbivore creatures made their home in the leaf litter of oak trees. In this endless simulator you take the role of a predator. Catch as many pinks as you can before 10 seconds have passed. You will notice that over a couple of generations your population of pinks will change in appearance, speed and size. Your preferences as a predator will determine the ultimate fate of these lovable creatures through the process known as evolution.
Available on Keats Island Conservation

Chronicles of Keats Island

In this educational, role player game based on Keats Island children learn the values of nature and the importance of conservation. Hyllus, a nature enthusiast decides to travel across Canada to visit Keats Island, a place filled with many unique species of plants and animals. But something is not right, and some unexpected events put in motion a race to save the island from destruction, caused by plastic pollution, logging and the introduction of invasive species to the island.
Available on Keats Island Conservation
